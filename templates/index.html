Features

The usual stuff...

Zelten lets you share messages with your friends, connect to new people, mention people, add hashtags, comment on messages... and pretty much all the other things you've come to expect from a social network.

Powered by Tent

Tent lets you use a whole range of apps with just a single account. All data produced and required by Tent-apps will be stored and fetched from your Tent account. This puts you in control of your own data.

Bookmarking

Zelten allows you to create bookmarks from URL's which you can either keep to yourself or share with your friends.

Connect to Twitter

SocialSync allows you to connect your Tent account to Twitter to automatically push your Tent status posts to Twitter.

Privacy

Except for authentication data (encrypted), cache data, and aggregate numbers, we save no data permanently. All your data will be stored in your account at your Tent provider of choice.

OpenSource

The application is on Github as well as the TentPHP client library that it uses internally. You can check our developer resources for information on the post types created for the Zelten product. Developers are free to use these post types in their projects.
