What is Tent.io?

Tent.io is decentralized social network. You own your data, not one central company. Your tent-data can be saved wherever you choose and you are always in full control who has access to your personal data. You can install your own tent server or use a free one, such as Tent.is.

Is this OpenSource?

Yes! The application is on Github as well as the TentPHP client library that is used internally. You can check our developer resources for information on the Zelten specific Tent.io Post and Profile Info Types.
