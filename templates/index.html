Tent.io what?

Tent.io is decentralized protocol to share and store data. A tent server acts a data-store for your personal data. You can install your own tent server or use a free one, such as Tent.is.

Bookmaks on Tent?

Zelten Bookmarks saves bookmarks on your tent server. You have full control over all the bookmark data. Except authentcation data Zelten saves no data itself.
